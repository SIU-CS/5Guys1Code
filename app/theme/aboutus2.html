About Us

The basis of the project is a simple organization application that the user can input appointments, meetings,
class assignments, and tests to get a simple calendar that will organize the upcoming work based on amount of
time to prepare (or complete) and a priority level set by the user. This is the most creative part of the application
in which we can create a system to prioritize and manage the user’s weekly load to help them with their organization
and remind them when they need to be studying or doing coursework that may have slipped their mind. The
target audience for this application is college students, but the idea will be transferred over to other fields like
full-time workers with minor adjustments. The idea really centers on college life and a student’s hectic
schedule and how to best manage their time. The motivation is an obvious one so we decided this would be a more
beneficial and useful application than those that have been thought of in the past. When it comes to more specific
functionalities we would like to touch on the prioritizing notifications concept, which will be the most useful and
difficult to design. The idea circles around setting up a system that can organize your coursework based on a
busy schedule. After entering your week of activities into a simple calendar with the option to have classes and work
reoccurring the user will begin submitting assignments and one-time events to the program. These events will be
prioritized based on a simple algorithm comparing the users set priority, completion time estimation, and due date.
This application is meant to take a simple calendar with reminders to the next level of organization.
